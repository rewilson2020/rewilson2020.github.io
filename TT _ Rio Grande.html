    • Our Process

      With state-of-the-art equipment at every stage of production, our juices, pulps, and oils are always top-of-the-line.

  • Citrus Grove

    Fresh fruit is harvested and transported from some of the best orchards in Texas.

    • Fruit Receiving / Weighing

      Every load of fruit is weighed at arrival where our traceability system starts right at the scale house.

    • Fruit Unloading

      Truck dumpers quickly unload all of the citrus.

    • Fruit Infeed

      Storage bins hold the incoming fruit before processing.

    • Fruit Washing

      An elevator moves the fresh fruit from washing to grading.

    • Fruit Grading

      An initial visual inspection of all fruit makes sure only the best continues on.

    • Fruit Sizing

      The citrus is sorted and sized to correctly feed the extractors.

    • Extraction

      The fruit moves through a fully automated JBT Juice Extraction System.

    • Oil Mill

      Cold-press oil is extracted and purified using JBT Extractors and centrifuges.

    • Juice Finishing

      Brown juice finishers ensure juice consistency and quality.

    • Juice Evaporation

      Finished juice is concentrated in TASTE evaporators.

    • Juice Blending

      Blended to customer specifications, all product is sampled and analyzed by quality assurance personnel.

    • Feed Mill

      Our zero-waste plant uses peels, seeds and pulp waste to make cattle feed.

  • Our Process

    With state-of-the-art equipment at every stage of production, our juices, pulps, and oils are always top-of-the-line.

Quality Control of
The Highest Quality.

From washing and grading to sizing and packing, our advanced citrus processing plant is quality controlled at every point of production.

Zero Waste, 
100% Great.

100% of our raw materials are used with absolutely zero waste. What’s left of our citrus (the peel, seeds and pulp) are turned into cattle feed.

  • Co-Packing

    At the end of the line, we offer packaging for all types of products. You can choose from a variety of materials like drums, bins, or bottles of glass or plastic. Then, we’ll pack it up perfectly with your design.

Storage

Storage

We’ll keep your juice on hand so you don’t have to ship it across the U.S.

Available storage units:

  • 25,000 sq. ft. Refrigerated Storage Warehouse (32-70℉)
  • 40,000 sq. ft. Frozen Storage Warehouse (0℉)
  • Aseptic tank storage for six million gallons of Not From Concentrate (NFC) juice to be fully operational by 2020.
  • We also utilize storage in The Netherlands at Kloosterboer for our European accounts.
